I'm also well-versed in UX design and mobile-first development (as you can see from this bitchin' website), and I also do research on various online topics, particularly about internet culture, community moderation, misinformation, political activism, internet censorship, platform policy/governance, DRM, online communities, online harassment, and the various intersections of mental health and social media.
